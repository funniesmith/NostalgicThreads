Questions

WHAT
YOU NEED
TO KNOW

Still unsure? Send a message below. We respond within 24 hours.

Records ship within 1–2 business days. Standard delivery runs 2–4 days domestically. International access typically arrives in 7–14 days depending on your location.

Free returns within 30 days of access. Items must be unworn, in original condition. Use our prepaid return label — no explanation needed. The archive holds no grudges.

S-I: Open Archive — available and reprintable. S-II: Restricted — limited, may return. S-III: Sealed — never reproduced. S-IV: Lost Record — destroyed, never shown again. Once a seal is closed, it doesn't open.

Every record includes a size guide with measurements in cm and inches. Our fit runs relaxed by default — if between sizes, size down for a closer fit. Fit codes are listed on each product (F-S = Soft fit, F-C = Classic).

GTB — Going To Be. It marks archived material that is pending full access or will be entering the archive soon. Watch for it. When it lands, it lands quietly.
